How Does RPL Works

Recognition of Prior Learning is a qualification pathway that helps convert your existing skills and knowledge into a recognised Australian Qualification.

1. Contact Client/student

Contact client and update sales tracking sheet

2. Checklist

If client is interested, send checklist according to desired/suggested course

3. Follow up

Help client in gathering documents and all related queries. Regularly update status on tracking sheet

4. Initial Assessment

Check all documents and work out eligibility of client for desired/suggested course.

5. Certification

If client is eligible for desired/suggested course, all required documents are validated by qualified assessors and certification is issued
